# AEVUM 2-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - 0:20 | Problem + Positioning
- "Most health apps track data. AEVUM turns biometrics into actionable, safety-aware daily protocols."
- Show login page and sign in with a real test account.

## 0:20 - 0:50 | Clinical Decision Layer
- Open **Insights**.
- Highlight risk/readiness/tier and protocol cards.
- Point out blocked or downgraded actions when contraindications are active.

## 0:50 - 1:15 | Safety + Versioning
- Toggle a contraindication and click **Apply Safety Profile**.
- Show immediate protocol updates.
- Call out **Change Intelligence** and **Version History** ("what changed and why").

## 1:15 - 1:35 | Adherence Intelligence
- Show adherence score, trend, projected risk delta, and action-level skip warnings.
- Explain this is where behavior prediction becomes intervention.

## 1:35 - 1:55 | Evidence Exports
- Click **Investor Pack**.
- Show outcomes table, version stability, adherence appendix.
- Mention this is designed for clinicians, pilots, and partners.

## 1:55 - 2:00 | Close
- "AEVUM is not just tracking recovery; it is operationalizing precision health decisions with safety and evidence."
