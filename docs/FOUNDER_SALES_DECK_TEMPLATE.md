# AEVUM Founder Sales Deck Template (12 Slides)

## Slide 1: Cover
- AEVUM
- "Safety-aware health intelligence for performance and care teams"
- Founder name, email, date

## Slide 2: Problem
- Teams struggle to turn biometrics into safe daily actions.
- Most tools track data but do not operationalize decisions.
- Compliance and audit readiness are weak in many wellness workflows.

## Slide 3: Solution
- AEVUM converts signals into daily protocol guidance.
- Safety layer blocks/downgrades contraindicated actions.
- Enterprise layer provides measurable cohort and ROI insights.

## Slide 4: Product Walkthrough
- Daily protocol engine
- Safety envelope + contraindication profile
- Clinician notes/signoff
- Compliance packaging + evidence export

## Slide 5: Why Now
- Wearable adoption is mainstream.
- Buyers demand measurable outcomes and compliance posture.
- Teams need operational health intelligence, not raw dashboards.
